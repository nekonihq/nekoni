# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our standards

**Expected behaviour:**
- Be respectful and constructive in issues, PRs, and discussions
- Give and accept feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the project and community

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Personal attacks, insults, or harassment of any kind
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or low-effort noise
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Issues and PRs that violate this code of conduct will be closed without discussion. Repeated violations may result in being blocked from the repository.

## Contact

If you need to report a conduct issue privately, open a blank issue and mention it's a conduct matter — we'll take it from there.
